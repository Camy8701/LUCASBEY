Everything you need to know before your osteopathy consultation. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

Do I need a doctor's prescription?

A prescription is generally not required for an osteopathy consultation. Some insurers may request one for reimbursement, so please check with your provider.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Bring any relevant medical information (imaging or reports) and wear comfortable clothing. Arrive a few minutes early if possible.

How long does a session last?

The duration varies depending on your consultation. We will confirm the expected length when you book.

Is osteopathy covered by insurance?

Coverage depends on your insurer or health plan. Please check your policy or contact us for guidance.

What languages do you speak?

Consultations are available in French, English, and Spanish.

How do I book an appointment?

Complete the booking form on the Contact page, call us at {{PHONE_DISPLAY}}, or book online via Doctena.

What is your cancellation policy?

Please let us know as early as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule so we can offer the slot to someone else.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Get in touch with us and we'll be happy to answer any questions you may have.
