Treatment Approach

Arthur believes in a holistic approach to rehabilitation, addressing not just the symptoms but the root cause of dysfunction. Each treatment plan is individualized, combining hands-on therapy with progressive exercise prescription.

Whether you're recovering from a sports injury, managing chronic pain, or looking to improve your physical performance, Arthur works with you to set realistic goals and track your progress every step of the way.
