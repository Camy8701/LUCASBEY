We offer a comprehensive range of physiotherapy services tailored to your individual needs. From sports injuries to chronic pain management, our evidence-based approach delivers lasting results.

healing hands

01

Back & Joints Rehabilitation

Comprehensive treatment for spinal disorders, herniated discs, sciatica, and joint pain. We combine manual therapy with targeted exercises to restore mobility, reduce pain, and prevent recurrence.

  • Spinal mobilization
  • Joint manipulation
  • Postural correction
  • Strengthening programs
02

Sports Physiotherapy

Specialized care for athletes at all levels. From acute injury management to performance optimization, we help you recover faster and perform better with sport-specific rehabilitation programs.

  • Injury rehabilitation
  • Return-to-sport programs
  • Performance screening
  • Preventive training
03

Lymphatic Drainage

Gentle, rhythmic manual technique that stimulates the lymphatic system to reduce swelling, detoxify the body, and promote healing. Particularly effective for post-surgical recovery and chronic conditions.

  • Edema reduction
  • Post-surgical recovery
  • Immune system support
  • Relaxation therapy
04

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including deep tissue massage, trigger point therapy, and myofascial release. Designed to relieve muscle tension, improve circulation, and accelerate natural healing.

  • Deep tissue work
  • Trigger point release
  • Myofascial techniques
  • Therapeutic massage

Insurance Coverage

With a valid prescription from your doctor, the CNS (Caisse Nationale de Sante) typically covers 70% of physiotherapy costs. You only pay 30% out of pocket. We can help you navigate the process.
