Everything you need to know about our physiotherapy services. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

Do I need a doctor's prescription?

In Luxembourg, you can see a physiotherapist directly without a prescription for an initial assessment. However, for insurance reimbursement (CNS), a medical prescription is required. We recommend obtaining one from your doctor for optimal coverage of your treatment costs.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring your medical prescription (if you have one), your CNS card, any relevant medical imaging (X-rays, MRI scans), and wear comfortable clothing that allows easy movement. Arrive 10 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

How long does a session last?

A standard physiotherapy session lasts 30-45 minutes. Your first appointment may take longer (up to 60 minutes) as we conduct a thorough assessment and discuss your treatment plan. The duration can vary depending on the type of treatment and your specific needs.

Is physiotherapy covered by insurance?

Yes, physiotherapy is partially covered by the CNS (Caisse Nationale de SantÃ©) in Luxembourg when you have a valid medical prescription. The reimbursement rate is typically 88% of the official tariff. We can provide you with the necessary documentation for your insurance claim.

What languages do you speak?

Our team is multilingual! We offer consultations in French, English, Portuguese, and Spanish. This allows us to serve Luxembourg's diverse international community effectively. Please let us know your preferred language when booking.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online through our Doctena booking system, call us directly at +352 691 385 556, or visit our contact page. We recommend booking online for the fastest response and to see real-time availability for all our practitioners.

What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask for 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the slot to other patients in need. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.

We're here to help. Get in touch with us and we'll be happy to answer any questions you may have.
